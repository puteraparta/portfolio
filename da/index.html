Supermarket Customer RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) Analysis

Data Scientist Program - 2024
Github

This is an RFM Analysis of a supermarket that offers products like wine, fruit, meat, fish, candy, and gold. They focus on delivering a high-quality shopping experience with multiple shopping options and promotional campaigns.

The company aims to boost profitability by analyzing customer data, such as purchase habits, campaign participation, and spending patterns.

The data reveals that some customers are highly engaged and contribute significantly to sales, while others are less active. By understanding these differences, the company can develop targeted strategies to improve sales and build stronger customer loyalty.
